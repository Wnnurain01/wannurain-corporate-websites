CONDITIONS

Child Lost Their Mother or Father

Children who have lost either mother or father or both can be accepted into the Orphans Welfare Organization in Mersing.

Age 7 Years Old - 18 Years Old

A person above 18 years old who lost their parents are not consider as orphans.

It is because they will be teach to survive their live.

Income Not Exceeding RM2,500

Child lost a parent but income's father or mother are not exceeding RM2,500 will be accepted.
