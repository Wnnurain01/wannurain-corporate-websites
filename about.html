About

MERSING ISLAMIC ORPHANS
WELFARE ORGANIZATION

Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Islam Mersing

Mersing Islamic Orphans Welfare Organization has been established since September 15, 1988 where it was founded by a line of Teachers and District Officer (Tuan Haji Muhammad Noah). Now, Tuan Haji Syah are responsible to Mersing Islamic Orphans Welfare Organization. This organization has a total of 14 employees consists of 10 staff and 4 wardens. In addition, this organization under Johor Welfare Organization.


Mersing Islamic Orphans Welfare Organization in Mersing only takes 50 to 55 children by several terms and conditions. It consists of 30 men and 25 women. They also provide several facilities such as sending childs to day schools and religious schools. If one of their childs want to continue study, they will help them to make a loan with Perbadanan Tabung Pendidikan Tinggi Nasional (Malaysia).


There are various activity carried out to fulfill the free time of the childrens under the care of this orphan welfare organization such as gotong-royong activity, cleaning the cemetery and evening sports. The responsible parties are very focused on a better lifestyle for the children when they are under their care.
